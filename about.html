At Crescent Integrated and General Merchandise Limited maintain quality and innovation in all our products. Ace Lubricants and Products Portfolio

Ace Lubricants is the flagship brand of Crescent Integrated and General Merchandise Limited, a prominent Nigerian company known for its high-quality industrial lubricants. The Ace Lubricants line is a testament to the company's commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. These products are meticulously designed to meet the diverse needs of the automotive and industrial sectors in Nigeria and beyond.

  1. ACE SPRINT SAE 40: is premium monograde engine oil for passenger cars, commercial vehicle, motor cycle, tricycles and all kind of generator. It is formulated with high refund virgin base oil and proven selected advance additives design to meet OEM specification lubricant requirement.
  2. SUPER HD 50 4L: is a superior quality monograde lubricant for diesel engines. It is blended from premium quality virgin base oil and formulated with highly advance additives with OEM specifications which provide best possible lubricant.
  3. ACE PLUS 20W 50: is a good quality multi grade with OEM specification. It provide exceptional control of deposits, wear, oil oxidation and excellent performance in a natural aspirated engines with good dispersant, detergent and soot control abilities.
  4. AH 68 HYDRAULIC Oil is formulated to satisfy rigorous operating applications with perfect control against wears and tears suitable for heavy duty engine and hydraulic machines
  5. SPEEDMAX 85W 140 Super Gear Oil is a lubricant made specifically for transmissions, transfer cases, and differentials in automobiles, trucks and other machinery.SPEEDMAX 85W 140 meets all required performance of:
    • Good Cleaning Ability
    • Low Fuel Consumption
    • Excellent Deposit Control
